# Core Features

## Patient (Flutter Mobile — PATIENT role)
- ✅ Registration with email, password, name, phone
- ✅ Login / Logout (JWT stored in SharedPreferences)
- ✅ Browse verified professionals (filter by city, specialization, rate)
- ✅ View professional detail + available slots
- ✅ Book a slot (HOME or CLINIC visit type)
- ✅ My Bookings — list and cancel bookings
- ❌ Reviews & ratings
- ❌ Chat with professional
- ❌ Payments

## Professional (Flutter Mobile — PROFESSIONAL role)
- ✅ Registration + profile completion in one step (specialization, license, city, rate)
- ✅ Account pending admin verification after registration
- ✅ Professional home dashboard (stub)
- ❌ Manage availability slots
- ❌ View patient bookings
- ❌ Care notes

## Admin (Angular Web — PLATFORM_ADMIN role)
- ✅ Login / Logout
- ✅ List all professionals (verified and unverified)
- ✅ Verify / unverify professionals
- ✅ Create, update, delete professionals
- ❌ Dispute management
- ❌ Analytics
